Her Ground joined forces with "Little Artist" at Government College University

A total of sixty (60) remarkable females participated in the workshop, including university students and underprivileged community women. The core objective of the workshop was to combat the stigma surrounding menstruation and openly discuss the challenges associated with unhygienic menstrual practices. Additionally, the event aimed to educate participants about sustainable menstrual product options, environmental protection, personal well-being, and the empowering process of creating reusable cloth pads at home.
